Find the best model

You will be given a random dataset, data, that contains the response y and 10 covariates labelled x1, x2, …, x10. Your task is to identify which of the covariates are important to predict the response. You can use any statistical technique you like to find the best model.

To make the game easier, you can increase the sample size.
